Create a professional logo that is memorable, versatile, and scalable.

Guidelines:
- The logo must look equally good at 32 × 32 px and at banner size.
- Prefer vector-clean shapes with no raster-style effects.
- Use at most 3 colours (including black/white).
- If text is included, use a clean geometric or humanist sans-serif style path.
- The mark and the logotype (if present) should be visually balanced.
- Leave breathing room between elements; avoid crowding.
- The logo should be immediately legible even at small sizes.
