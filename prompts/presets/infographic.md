Create a visually engaging infographic that communicates information clearly.

Guidelines:
- Combine icons, charts, and short text labels to tell a story with data.
- Use a clear visual hierarchy: headline → key stats → supporting detail.
- Group related content inside visual containers (cards, banded rows, or circles).
- Use colour consistently to encode categories or values.
- Keep individual text labels short (≤ 5 words).
- Include at least one data visualisation element (bar chart, pie chart, or progress arc).
- Leave enough whitespace between sections to avoid feeling crowded.
