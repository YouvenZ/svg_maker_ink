Create a seamless repeating pattern that tiles perfectly in all four directions.

Guidelines:
- The motif must align exactly at the left/right edges and the top/bottom edges.
- Use a `<defs>` block with a `<pattern>` element and reference it via `fill="url(#...)"`.
- Keep the tile motif relatively simple so it reads well when repeated.
- Choose a background colour and at least one accent colour for the motif.
- The overall result should feel rhythmic and balanced when tiled across a large surface.
- Avoid motifs that are overly complex or hard to distinguish at smaller sizes.
