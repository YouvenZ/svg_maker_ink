name: Icon
description: Optimised instructions for generating UI/UX icons.
category: preset
---
Create a simple, immediately recognisable icon designed for UI/UX use.

Guidelines:
- Use clear, bold shapes with minimal detail.
- The design must read clearly at 16 × 16 px and scale up to 512 × 512 px.
- Prefer solid geometric forms over complex paths.
- Limit the colour palette to 1–3 colours.
- Avoid text labels inside the icon.
- Leave a small amount of padding inside the viewBox so the icon does not clip.
