---
name: Minimal
description: Clean, stripped-back aesthetic with generous whitespace.
category: style
---
Use a minimal, clean design. Remove anything that is not structurally essential.

- Generous whitespace around and between elements.
- Simple, bold shapes — no decorative flourishes.
- A restrained colour palette (1–2 colours plus white/black).
- No gradients, no textures, no drop shadows.
- Every element must earn its place.
