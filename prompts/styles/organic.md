---
name: Organic / Natural
description: Flowing curves, natural forms, nothing perfectly geometric.
category: style
---
Use organic, natural, flowing shapes. Avoid anything perfectly geometric or mechanical.

- All forms should use smooth Bézier curves (`C`, `Q`, `S` path commands).
- Shapes may be irregular, asymmetric, and imperfect — like objects found in nature.
- Silhouettes of leaves, water, stones, clouds, or living creatures are good reference points.
- Colours should feel natural (earth tones, sky blues, leaf greens) unless overridden.
