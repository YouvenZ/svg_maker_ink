---
name: Flat
description: Flat design with solid colours, no shadows, no gradients.
category: style
---
Use strict flat design principles.

- Solid colours only — no gradients, no drop shadows, no embossing.
- Simple geometric shapes with hard, clean edges.
- Bold colour contrasts between adjacent areas.
- Typography (if present) is clean sans-serif.
- No 3D effects, no bevels, no gloss.
