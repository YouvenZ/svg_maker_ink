---
name: Geometric
description: Precise geometric shapes and mathematical patterns.
category: style
---
Use only geometric shapes: circles, rectangles, triangles, regular polygons, and straight lines.

- All forms are precise and angular — no freehand curves or irregular paths.
- Relationships between shapes (alignment, proportion, symmetry) should be mathematically clean.
- Grids, tessellations, and rotational symmetry are encouraged.
- No organic or hand-drawn elements.
