---
name: Hand Drawn / Sketchy
description: Imperfect, gestural, hand-drawn appearance.
category: style
---
Give the artwork a hand-drawn, sketchy, gestural quality.

- Lines should appear slightly imperfect — simulate natural hand movement.
- Use `stroke-linecap="round"` and `stroke-linejoin="round"`.
- Add small irregularities to paths rather than using perfectly smooth curves.
- Cross-hatching or loose shading is encouraged for tonal areas.
- The overall impression should feel like a confident sketch rather than a polished vector.
