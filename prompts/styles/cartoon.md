---
name: Cartoon / Comic
description: Bold outlines, flat colours, and expressive cartoon style.
category: style
---
Use a cartoon or comic-book illustration style.

- Bold, uniform outlines (stroke-width 2–4 px) on all major shapes.
- Flat, bright fill colours with no gradients on primary shapes.
- Simple highlight spots (small white ellipses at ~70% opacity) for a glossy feel.
- Expressive, slightly exaggerated proportions and forms.
- Black outlines (`stroke="#000"`) are the primary visual separator.
- Speech bubbles, action lines, or halftone textures are welcome if thematically appropriate.
