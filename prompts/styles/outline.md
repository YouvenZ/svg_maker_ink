---
name: Outline / Line Art
description: Pure line art — strokes only, no fills.
category: style
---
Use a pure line-art / outline style. Strokes only — no filled areas.

- All shapes use `fill="none"` with a visible `stroke` colour.
- Vary stroke-width to suggest depth: thicker outer contours, thinner inner details.
- Use `stroke-linecap="round"` and `stroke-linejoin="round"` for a polished look.
- No gradients or solid fills — the white background shows through all shapes.
- The composition should read clearly as a line drawing.
