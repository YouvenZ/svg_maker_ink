---
name: Filled Shapes
description: Filled silhouettes with no or minimal strokes.
category: style
---
Use filled shapes as the primary visual language. Strokes are optional and minimal.

- All forms defined by their fill colour, not their outline.
- `stroke="none"` on most elements; a subtle stroke (≤ 1 px) is acceptable only where it improves legibility.
- Use colour contrast between adjacent fills to separate shapes instead of outlines.
- Silhouettes and negative space are key compositional tools.
