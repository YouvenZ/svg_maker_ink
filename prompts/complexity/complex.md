Create a richly complex, highly detailed design.

- 30+ distinct shapes or path segments are expected.
- Multiple layers of detail: structural forms, secondary elements, fine line work, textures, and highlights.
- Use `<g>` groups to organise layers logically.
- Fine details such as cross-hatching, micro-patterns, or intricate path work are encouraged.
- The design should feel lush and reward careful examination.
