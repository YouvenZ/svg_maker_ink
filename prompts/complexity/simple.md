---
name: Simple
description: Minimal element count — easy to read at a glance.
category: complexity
---
Keep the design simple and uncluttered.

- Use the fewest elements necessary to communicate the subject.
- No more than 5–10 distinct shapes in total.
- Favour large, bold, easy-to-read forms.
- No fine detail, texture, or decorative embellishment.
- The design should be instantly recognisable even as a thumbnail.
