---
name: Medium Strokes
description: Balanced, general-purpose stroke weight.
category: stroke
---
Use medium-weight strokes.

- Default stroke-width: 1.5–2.5 px.
- A versatile weight that works for most illustration and icon styles.
- Use consistent stroke-width across similar elements for visual cohesion.
- `stroke-linejoin="round"` for a friendly feel; `miter` for precision/technical work.
