---
name: Variable Stroke Width
description: Calligraphic lines that vary in weight along their length.
category: stroke
---
Use variable, calligraphic stroke widths that change along the length of each line.

- Simulate the look of a brush or calligraphy pen.
- Thick at curves and stroke starts; thin at tapers and stroke ends.
- Achieve this with SVG path shapes (filled areas) rather than actual strokes, or use `stroke-width` changes on separate path segments.
- The variation in weight should feel intentional and expressive, not random.
- This style suits logos, lettering, and organic illustrations.
