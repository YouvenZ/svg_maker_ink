---
name: Thick Strokes
description: Bold, heavy lines for emphasis and impact.
category: stroke
---
Use thick, bold strokes.

- Default stroke-width: 3–6 px (scale relative to canvas size).
- Bold lines create a graphic, high-impact appearance.
- Inner detail lines may be slightly thinner than primary contours for hierarchy.
- `stroke-linecap="round"` and `stroke-linejoin="round"` to avoid sharp spike artefacts.
