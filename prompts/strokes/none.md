---
name: No Stroke
description: Fills only — no outlines on any element.
category: stroke
---
Do not use any strokes. All elements are defined purely by their fill colour.

- Set `stroke="none"` on every shape element.
- Use colour contrast between adjacent fills to separate shapes.
- Silhouettes and negative space are your primary compositional tools.
- No border, outline, or line should appear anywhere in the design.
