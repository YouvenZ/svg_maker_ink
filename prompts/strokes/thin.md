---
name: Thin Strokes
description: Delicate, lightweight lines.
category: stroke
---
Use thin, delicate strokes throughout.

- Default stroke-width: 0.5–1 px.
- `stroke-linecap="round"` and `stroke-linejoin="round"` for elegance.
- Thin lines create a refined, precise, technical appearance.
- Ensure sufficient contrast between stroke colour and background so lines remain visible.
