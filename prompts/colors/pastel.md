---
name: Pastel
description: Soft, desaturated pastel tones.
category: color
---
Use a soft pastel colour palette.

- All colours should be low-saturation, high-lightness tints.
- The overall mood should feel gentle, airy, and delicate.
- Use a variety of pastel hues (pink, lavender, mint, peach, sky blue, soft yellow).
- Avoid dark shadows or high-contrast colour pairings.
- White or very pale grey works well as a background.
