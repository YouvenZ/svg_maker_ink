---
name: Grayscale
description: Black, white, and shades of grey only.
category: color
---
Use a pure grayscale palette.

- Only black, white, and shades of grey are permitted (`#000`, `#fff`, and hex greys such as `#888`).
- Use tonal contrast to separate shapes and create depth.
- At least four distinct grey values should be present.
- No colour hues of any kind.
