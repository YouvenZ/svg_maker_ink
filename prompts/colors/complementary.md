---
name: Complementary
description: Two hues directly opposite on the colour wheel for maximum contrast.
category: color
---
Use a complementary colour scheme.

- Choose exactly two hues that sit directly opposite each other on the colour wheel (e.g., blue and orange, red and green, purple and yellow).
- Use one hue as dominant (≈ 70% of coloured areas) and the other as accent (≈ 30%).
- Vary lightness and saturation within each hue to add tonal range.
- The contrast between the two hues should create visual energy and clear focal points.
