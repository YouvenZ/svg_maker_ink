---
name: Earth Tones
description: Natural, muted palette inspired by soil, rock, and foliage.
category: color
---
Use an earthy, natural colour palette.

- Draw colours from soil, stone, sand, wood, and dried foliage: browns, tans, ochres, terracotta, olive greens, dusty blues.
- Colours should be desaturated and muted rather than vivid.
- Avoid bright or neon colours.
- The palette should evoke natural landscapes and organic materials.
