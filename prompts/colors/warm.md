---
name: Warm Colours
description: Palette drawn from the warm half of the colour wheel.
category: color
---
Use a warm colour palette exclusively.

- Restrict all colours to the warm half of the colour wheel: reds, oranges, yellows, and red-browns.
- You may include off-whites and creams as neutrals.
- Avoid cool hues (blue, cyan, green, purple).
- Vary warmth and saturation within the palette to avoid monotony.
