---
name: Vibrant
description: Bold, fully saturated, high-energy colours.
category: color
---
Use a vibrant, high-energy colour palette.

- Colours should be bold and fully saturated (HSL saturation ≥ 80%).
- Use strong contrast between adjacent colours for visual impact.
- Include at least 3 distinct hues spread across the colour wheel.
- The result should feel energetic and attention-grabbing.
- Avoid muted, desaturated, or earthy tones.
