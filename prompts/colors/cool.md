---
name: Cool Colours
description: Palette drawn from the cool half of the colour wheel.
category: color
---
Use a cool colour palette exclusively.

- Restrict all colours to the cool half of the colour wheel: blues, cyans, blue-greens, and purples.
- You may include white and light greys as neutrals.
- Avoid warm hues (red, orange, yellow).
- Use variations in saturation and lightness to create visual interest.
