---
name: Neon / Glow
description: Bright neon colours with a dark background glow effect.
category: color
---
Use a neon/glow colour palette on a dark background.

- Background should be very dark (near-black: `#0a0a0a` to `#1a1a2e`).
- Accent colours should be electric and highly saturated: neon pink, cyan, electric green, hot orange, bright purple.
- Simulate a glow effect using SVG `<filter>` with `feGaussianBlur` and `feComposite`.
- Use white or near-white at full brightness for the sharpest highlights.
- The overall look should feel futuristic, cyberpunk, or retro-arcade.
