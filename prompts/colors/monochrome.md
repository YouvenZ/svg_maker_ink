---
name: Monochrome
description: One hue in a range of shades, tints, and tones.
category: color
---
Use a strictly monochromatic colour palette.

- Choose a single hue and vary only its lightness and saturation.
- Use at least three distinct tonal values (light, mid, dark) to create contrast.
- White and very light tints count as part of the palette.
- No other hues are permitted.
